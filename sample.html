About Yenepoya University

Yenepoya University, located in Mangalore, Karnataka, India, is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in education, research, and healthcare. Established in 2008, it is recognized as a Deemed-to-be University under the Ministry of Education, Government of India.

The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including medicine, dentistry, nursing, allied health sciences, and management. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a commitment to innovation, Yenepoya University strives to nurture future leaders and professionals.

Yenepoya University is also known for its emphasis on community service and ethical practices, fostering a culture of compassion and social responsibility among its students and staff.
